How we determine our group recommendation:
  1. We have designed and implemented nine algorithms for this recommendation.
    • Additive Utilitarian
    • Multiplicative Utilitarian
    • Borda Count
    • Copend Rule
    • Plurality Voting
    • Approval Voting
    • Least Misery
    • Most Pleasure
    • Average without Misery
  2. All nine algorithms are run at once. Then the top results are then taken from each algorithm.
  3. Then the movie that has occurred the most from the individual results is determined as the most suitable movie.
Choice 1
Complete form on one account
Group
  1. This feature requires all users participating in the group feature to complete a form on one account.
  2. Please specify how many people are in the group, that will be voting on their preferred movie.
  3. Then please select the movies that you would like to watch. Note, each member of the group can only select a maximum of one movie. E.g. If there are 3 people in the group, then only a maximum of 3 movies can be selected. Then confirm your selection by clicking "confirm".
  4. In the table provided, each user should assign a preference score from 1 to 10 across all movies. 10 being the most preferred movie and 1 being the least preferred movie. Then confirm your inputs by clicking "submit".
Choice 2
Complete form on individual accounts
Group chat
  1. This feature requires all users participating in the group feature to complete a form on their individual accounts.
  2. One person in the group must create a chat room and specify how many people in the group are going to vote. An infinite amount of people can join a chat room but the amount of people who are voting must be specified.
  3. When a room is created, the other group members must join the chat room by entering the room number into the form field.
  4. Once all voters have entered the room, a list of all movies in the cinema will appear.
  5. Then please select the movies that you would like to watch. Note, each member of the group must select one movie.
  6. In the table provided, each user should assign a preference score from 1 to 10 across all movies. 10 being the most preferred movie and 1 being the least preferred movie. Then confirm your inputs by clicking "submit".
  7. Once everyone has left a chat room, it will automatically be deleted.
  8. Note, once the form has been completed, please wait for all users to complete their form before clicking the recommendation button.
